Why is ARIA funding essential for the success of the project?

Panagiotis Kouvaros, one of the key researchers is based in Cyprus. There are limited options for getting a joint funding between the UK and Cyprus. This ARIA funding is essential to making this work happen.


What key milestones will mark your proposed projects progress?

M1: The programming language, the intermediate language and the translation from the former to the latter.

M2: A working end-to-end NS-MAS verification tool with a MILP backend.
